  • FAQ

We create inspiring interiors that combine comfort, functionality, and timeless design. Every space is thoughtfully tailored to reflect your lifestyle and needs.

  • What interior design services do you offer?
    We provide residential and commercial interior design, space planning, concept development, material selection, and full project styling.
    How do I start a design project with you?
    Simply contact us or request a consultation. We’ll discuss your needs, style preferences, and project scope before starting.
    Do you handle both residential and commercial spaces?
    Yes, we design homes, offices, retail spaces, and hospitality interiors tailored to each client’s goals and brand identity.
    What is included in your design process?
    Our process includes concept design, mood boards, layout planning, 3D visualization, material selection, and final styling.
    How long does an interior design project take?
    Timelines vary by scope. Small projects may take weeks, while full renovations or large interiors can take several months.
    Do you provide furniture and decor sourcing?
    Yes, we assist with selecting and sourcing furniture, lighting, materials, and decor to match your design vision.
    Can you work with existing furniture or layouts?
    Absolutely. We can redesign spaces by incorporating existing elements while improving layout and overall aesthetics.
    Do you offer renovation and space planning services?
    Yes, we provide renovation guidance, layout optimization, and space planning to improve functionality and flow.
    What design styles do you specialize in?
    We specialize in modern, minimalist, contemporary, and luxury interiors tailored to your personal taste.
    Do you provide 3D renderings or visual previews?
    Yes, we create detailed 3D visualizations so you can preview the final result before execution begins.
  • How do I track the progress of my project?
    We provide regular updates, design revisions, and progress reports throughout each stage of the project.
    Do you provide ongoing communication during projects?
    Yes, we maintain clear communication via meetings, messaging, or email to ensure alignment at every stage.
    What if I want to revise the design concept?
    Revisions are part of our process. We refine the design based on your feedback to achieve the best result.
    Can I make changes during project execution?
    Yes, adjustments are possible, though some changes may affect timelines or costs depending on the scope.
    Do you offer support after project completion?
    Yes, we provide post-project support, including styling advice and minor adjustments if needed.
    Can I schedule consultations before committing?
    Yes, we offer initial consultations to understand your needs before starting the project.
    Do you collaborate with architects or contractors?
    Yes, we work closely with architects, builders, and contractors to ensure smooth project execution.
    What information do you need to begin?
    We need your space details, preferences, budget range, and any inspiration references you have.
    Can someone represent me during the project?
    Yes, a representative can coordinate with us, provided they understand your design goals and approvals.
    What if issues arise during the project?
    We handle challenges proactively and coordinate solutions to keep your project on track.
  • What pricing options do you offer?
    We offer flexible pricing based on project scope, including fixed packages or customized design fees.
    Do you provide detailed cost estimates?
    Yes, we provide clear cost breakdowns covering design, materials, and execution phases.
    How are materials and furniture costs handled?
    Costs depend on your selections. We help you choose options that align with your budget and style.
    Can I request a customized design package?
    Absolutely. We tailor our services based on your needs, whether partial styling or full interior design.
    Do you work within specific budgets?
    Yes, we design solutions that maximize value while staying within your defined budget.
    Are there any additional or hidden fees?
    No, we maintain transparency and outline all costs clearly before the project begins.
    Do you offer payment plans?
    Yes, payment can be structured in phases based on project milestones.
    Can I access project documents and plans?
    Yes, all design files, layouts, and documents are shared with you throughout the project.
    Do you manage contractors and vendors?
    Yes, we coordinate with vendors and contractors to ensure design accuracy and quality execution.
    Do you offer turnkey interior solutions?
    Yes, we provide end-to-end services from concept to final installation for a seamless experience.
