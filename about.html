About Us

We’re committed to turning your vision into reality

Founded by a passion for innovation and excellence, Blanc Design stands as one of the leading names in architecture, exterior and interior design. Since 2020, we have been delivering creative and functional design solutions, combining aesthetic vision with practical execution. Backed by a fully equipped factory established in 2012, we not only design but also manufacture custom-made furniture and interior elements to ensure every project reflects quality and precision. Whether it's residential, commercial, or bespoke interior projects, we are committed to creating spaces that are both inspiring and timeless.
